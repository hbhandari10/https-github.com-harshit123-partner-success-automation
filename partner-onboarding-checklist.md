# Partner Onboarding Checklist

## Step 1: Initial Setup
- Collect partner details
- Define goals and expectations

## Step 2: Training
- Product walkthrough
- Sales process alignment

## Step 3: Enablement
- Share sales materials
- Provide CRM access

## Step 4: Go-Live
- First deal support
- Regular check-ins

## Step 5: Optimization
- Performance tracking
- Feedback loop
